Spaghetti with Beef and Vegetables

Warm up with this spicy tomato sauce featuring ground beef and fresh vegetables.

Ingredients
8 oz Dry spaghetti, uncooked
1/2 lb Ground sirloin beef
1/4 cup Chopped onion
1 can Tomato Sauce
1 tbsp Red wine vinegar
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1/2 tsp Granulated sugar
1/4 tsp Garlic powder
1/4 tsp Crushed red pepper flakes
1 cup Thinly sliced zucchini
1 cup Medium tomatoes, seeded, chopped
3 tbsp Chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions, omitting salt. Drain; set aside.
  2. Combine meat and onions in medium non-stick skillet. Cook over medium heat 4 to 5 minutes, or until meat is no longer pink, stirring frequently. Drain, if necessary.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, vinegar, Italian seasoning, sugar, garlic powder and red pepper. Cook 3 to 4 minutes, or until heated through, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add pasta and zucchini; mix well. Cook 2 to 3 minutes, or until heated through, stirring occasionally. Stir in tomatoes and parsley; heat through.
